About Honeypot Hill Publishing

Honeypot Hill Publishing was conceived out of Reina’s inspiration to start writing stories for young children whilst she was in hospital recovering from breast cancer surgery.  She had an overwhelming feeling that something good should come out of her situation and she is delighted to announce the release of her first book, SKYLA AND THE SNOWFLAKE FAIRY.

Reina’s daughter is her inspiration; not only for ideas but also due to her daughter’s love of books and their precious story time together at bedtime which they are never allowed to miss!

Many of Reina’s stories deal with issues and situations which she and her daughter have experienced personally, and she hopes that they will resonate with many other children and parents.

Honeypot Hill Publishing aims to create beautiful rhyming stories about life experiences seen through the eyes of young children.  The intention is to help children develop self-belief and a deeper understanding of the experiences they, or their friends, might be going through.

We hope you and your children enjoy reading our first publication, SKYLA AND THE SNOWFLAKE FAIRY which you can buy here.

Honeypot Hill Publishing is also keen to do its part to help safeguard our environment so all the packaging used for shipping orders, placed via Ebay, are eco-friendly and biodegradable.

  • ABOUT THE AUTHOR

    Reina Bonici-Mompalao-Lee

    Reina Bonici-Mompalao-Lee calls herself a true European; half English, half Maltese and born in The Netherlands, having lived in the UK, Malta, Germany and The Netherlands. She lives with her husband and young daughter in Buckinghamshire, surrounded by the beautiful Chiltern Hills, having moved there from South-East London in search of a slower pace of life.

    Career

    As a graduate, Reina embarked on a career in media and broadcasting which has spanned 23 years and has provided her the opportunity of working in both radio and television. Learn more.

    Hobbies

    When Reina’s not working, doing the school run or focusing on her new love of writing books, she can be found swimming laps of the pool, reading and going for country walks with her husband and daughter. Learn more.
